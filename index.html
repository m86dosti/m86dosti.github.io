Accelerator
NAMAKDAN

Namakdan Accelerator is the first technical and professional accelerator in the country, established with the aim of educating, empowering, and supporting concerned elites.

It operates with the slogan of innovation, skill training, and knowledge-based businesses in the field of education, empowerment, and acceleration of start-up companies in line with the boom in production, job creation, entrepreneurship, and the creation of knowledge-based businesses, in line with the macro strategy of the resistance economy and with the long-term vision of a strong Iran.
